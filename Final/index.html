The Story Behind Chic City Eats

This project turns restaurant reviews into a collaborative, visual story of the city. Instead of a static list, Chic City Eats focuses on trends, vibes, and community contributions. Every card, every star, and every bar in the chart comes from real people adding their favorite spots and experiences.

From a systems perspective, the app uses a RESTful Express server, a SQLite database with Sequelize models, and a lightweight frontend that talks to the API to keep the interface live and interactive.
