The Story Behind Chic City Eats

This project turns restaurant reviews into a collaborative, data-driven moodboard of the city. Instead of long essays, Chic City Eats focuses on trends, vibes, and community patterns.

From a systems perspective, the app uses a RESTful Express server, a SQLite database powered by Sequelize models, and a lightweight frontend that talks to the API. Every card, rating, and bar in the chart comes directly from the database.

The idea is to feel more like a digital magazine spread than a typical CRUD app, while still checking all the boxes for routes, persistence, and visualization.
