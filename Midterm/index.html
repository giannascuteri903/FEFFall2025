Spotlight Restaurant of the Month

Nobu Miami

This month I visited the resturant at the Nobu Hotel. It featured sophisticated Japanese-Peruvian dining on Miami's glamorous oceanfront. Nobu blends iconic flavors with stunning presentation and coastal ambiance. The food, vibe, and service were all exceptional.
